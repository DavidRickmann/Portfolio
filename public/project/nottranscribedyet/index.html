Birmingham Metro Eastside Extension Business Case Transport For West Midlands

David worked as data scientist on the Birmingham Metro Eastside Extension Business Case. This involved developing methods for processing very large delay matrices into a format that could then be modelled by the economics team.

2012

Thameslink Systems Modelling and Integration Year / Location 2008-2012, UK Client Position Held Network Rail Engineer

Role and Outcome: As an engineer David was responsible for across the board performance and integration analysis of all parts of the Thameslink project. This involved performance analysis of numerous schemes ranging from the adjustment of individual junctions to the signalling of London Bridge approach. Whilst at Thameslink David developed new modelling methods for fully automated mainline rail services, dwell time algorithms and led the development team of modelling software.

Relevance: Experience with performance modelling on large scale development projects
